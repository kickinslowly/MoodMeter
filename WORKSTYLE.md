# Workstyle

## Prioritization Criteria

**Primary**: Deep quality — things should work perfectly before shipping more
**Secondary**: UX polish — feel matters, make it addictive and visually satisfying
**Tertiary**: Broad feature coverage — new features after existing ones are solid
**Tie-breaker**: Quick wins first (lower effort wins when impact is equal)

## Tone & Identity
- Neutral, silly, fun, engaging, modern, cool
- Memes everywhere — Gen-Z slang, internet culture, brainrot humor
- Target audience: students playing in free time at school or home (13+)

## Competitors
- Poki games (free browser games, instant play, addictive loops)
- Any free/fun website students find online and get hooked on
- Benchmark: can a student open this instead of Poki and stay engaged?

## Pivot Rules
- Stuck 3 attempts on same approach → switch to different task or reframe
- Repeated blocker on high-priority goal → force alternative path
- Competitor clearly ahead in specific area → analyze their approach, leapfrog not parity

## Post-Ship Checklist
- Does this match or beat competitor engagement?
- Any discovered work to log in GOALS.md?
- Is consolidation due?
